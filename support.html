Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new habit to track?

+

To add a new habit, tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the home screen. Enter your habit name, select a category (optional), and set your quit date. Tap "Save" to start tracking your clear days.

What happens if I relapse?

+

We understand that setbacks happen. If you need to reset your counter, simply swipe right on the habit card and confirm the reset. Remember, every journey has ups and downs - what matters is getting back on track.

Is my data private?

+

Absolutely. Clear Days stores all data locally on your device. We don't have servers, don't collect any personal information, and can't see your habits or progress. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption.

How do widgets work?

+

Widgets let you see your progress right from your home screen. To add a widget, long-press on your home screen, tap the "+" button, search for Clear Days, and choose your preferred widget size. You can customize which habits appear in the widget.

Can I export my data?

+

Yes! You can export your progress data at any time. Go to Settings and tap "Export Data" to save your habit tracking history as a file you can keep or share.

How do I delete a habit?

+

To delete a habit, swipe left on the habit card on the home screen and confirm deletion. This action cannot be undone, so make sure you want to permanently remove the habit and its data.

What do the milestone badges mean?

+

Milestone badges celebrate your progress at key intervals: 1 day, 1 week, 2 weeks, 1 month, 2 months, 3 months, 6 months, and 1 year. Each milestone represents a significant achievement in breaking your habit.

Does the app work offline?

+

Yes! Clear Days works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection. If you use iCloud sync, changes will sync when you're back online.
