Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Clear Days is designed with privacy at its core.

Last Updated: January 2025

ðŸ”’ Privacy First Design

Clear Days stores all your data locally on your device. We don't have servers, we don't track you, and we can't see your habits or progress. Your journey is yours alone.

1. Information Collection

Clear Days is designed to respect your privacy:

  • No Account Required: You can use Clear Days without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Local Storage: All habit data, including names, dates, and progress, is stored locally on your device using Core Data.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
  • No Ads: Clear Days is ad-free and always will be.

2. Data Storage

Local Data

Your habit tracking data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Habit names and categories
  • Quit dates and progress tracking
  • Milestone achievements
  • App preferences and settings

3. Data Sharing

We have a simple policy: We don't share your data because we don't have it.

  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is sold or used for marketing
  • No data leaves your device unless you explicitly share it

4. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Export your progress data at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual habits or all data instantly
  • Reset: Start fresh whenever you want
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all local data

5. Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS's built-in security features
  • Device passcode/biometric protection
  • Encrypted device storage
  • Optional iCloud end-to-end encryption

6. Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • They are scheduled locally on your device
  • No data is sent to push notification servers
  • You can customize or disable them anytime

7. Widgets

Home screen widgets:

  • Display data stored on your device
  • Update locally without network requests
  • Respect your privacy settings

8. Children's Privacy

Clear Days does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app stores all data locally and requires no personal information to function.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you through the app if changes are significant
  • Always maintain our commitment to privacy-first design

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy or how Clear Days works:

  • Email: dev.blsmdrive@gmail.com
  • Visit our Support page

Our Promise

Clear Days will always be private by design. We believe your journey to breaking bad habits is personal, and your data should remain that way.
